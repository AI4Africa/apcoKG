Restricted tenderc back to ToC or Class ToC

IRI: http://data.apco.cm/onto#AppelDOffreRestreint

Restricted call for tenders is now addressed to all candidates selected following a pre-qualification procedure and no longer to a limited number of candidates among those pre-qualified. The pre-qualification report, drawn up by the Contracting Authority or the Contracting Authority's Representative and accompanied by the draft tender dossier with the proposal for shortlists, is submitted to the competent procurement commission for examination. The restricted tendering procedure may only be used in the following cases: (1) specific works or equipment of great importance or complexity; (2) specialized supplies and services; (3) intellectual services.
